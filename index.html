MELANY SANCHEZ

Exploring the current state of the world through music.

"I WANT TO BE ODD"

Melany Sanchez is a songwriter, singer, pianist, and producer from Colombia who fuses Latin jazz with creative lyrics and comedic storytelling, drawing strong influences from musical theater. She delivers captivating performances that transport audiences into her unique perspective on everyday life, featuring fast-paced piano melodies and intriguing harmonies. She seeks to create a deep connection with her audience, inviting them to relate to her stories and share a laugh along the way.
